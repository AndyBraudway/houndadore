Exercise is crucial for a Houndadore’s physical and mental well-being. However, during hot weather, it is important to adjust their exercise routine accordingly. Avoid exercising your Houndadore during the hottest parts of the day, typically between 10 am and 4 pm. Instead, opt for early morning or late evening walks when the temperatures are cooler. Additionally, be mindful of the pavement temperature. Asphalt and concrete can become scorching hot and burn your Houndadore’s paws. To prevent this, walk your dog on grassy areas or invest in protective booties.<\/p>\n

Grooming plays a significant role in keeping your Houndadore comfortable in hot weather. Regular brushing helps remove excess fur and prevents matting, allowing air to circulate through their coat. Heavy panting, excessive drooling, rapid heartbeat, and weakness are all indicators that your Houndadore may be suffering from heatstroke.
