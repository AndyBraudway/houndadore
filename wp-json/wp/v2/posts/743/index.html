One of the most common health issues in Houndadores is obesity. These dogs have a hearty appetite and can easily gain weight if not properly managed. Ear infections are another common health issue in Houndadores. Their floppy ears can trap moisture and debris, creating a breeding ground for bacteria and yeast. To prevent ear infections, it is important to regularly clean and dry your Houndadore’s ears. Regular grooming and keeping the ears dry after swimming or bathing can also help prevent infections.<\/p>\n

Regular grooming, including bathing with hypoallergenic shampoos, can help alleviate skin irritations. If your Houndadore has food allergies, work with your veterinarian to find a suitable diet that eliminates the allergen.<\/p>\n
